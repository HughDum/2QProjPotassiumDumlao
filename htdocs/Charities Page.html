Going Nuclear and Beyond

Why should you donate to a charity?

Photo by Mika Baumeister on Unsplash

Other than just preventing Climate Change through your small actions like recycling or planting trees around your house, you can also donate money to Charities dedicated to stopping Climate Change. Donating is a great way to promote and help those who are actively trying to stop global warming. It helps them do what they can to help, and in return you feel satisfied with an improved sense of wellbeing.

List of 10 Climate Change Related Charities:

  • Clean Air Task Force: Donate Here

    The Clean Air Task Force aims to create a zero-emissions, high-energy planet at an affordable cost by pushing changes in technologies and policies. They want to create a world where the energy needs of all are met without damaging the atmosphere. They focus on advancing nuclear technology, redirecting bioenergy and land use, accelerating carbon capture technologies, reorienting energy access to support Africa and beyond, removing fossil power plants, investing in superhot rock geothermal technology, advancing zero-carbon fuels, and global reduction on super pollutants.

  • Climate Emergency Fund: Donate Here

    The Climate Emergency Fund supports young, ambitious organizations that tell the truth about the climate emergency, disrupt normalcy, organize mass protests, and demand transformative change by funding them. They provide safe and legal means for donors to support activists and ensure funds support high impact projects, groups, and individuals.

  • American Nuclear Society: Donate Here

    The American Nuclear Society is the premier organization for those that embrace the nuclear sciences and technologies for thier contributions to improving lives and preserving the Earth. They aim to advance the development of nuclear science, engineering, and technology to benefit society.

  • Nuclear Matters: Donate Here

    Nuclear matters is a national coalition of advocates working to inform the public and policymakers about the benefits of nuclear energy. They support policy solutions that properly value nuclear energy as a reliable, affordable, safe, and carbon-free energy source.

  • Coalition for Rainforest Nations: Donate Here

    The Coalition for Rainforest Nations seek responsible stewardship of the world's last great rainforests through innovative strategies. They seek to create lasting opportunities for reform in a manner that enhances tropical forest stewardship, biodiversity conservation and global climate stability by reversing the destruction of tropical rainforests.

  • Information Technology and Innovation Foundation: Donate Here

    ITIF is a nonprofit research and educational institute. Its mission is to formulate, evaluate, and promote policy solutions that accelerate innovation and boost productivity. It's goal is to provide policymakers with high quality information, analysis, and recommendations they can trust.

  • Environmental Defense Fund: Donate Here

    The Environmental Defense Fund preserves the natural habitats of living animals. The team also works alongside government bodies and communities. They focus on climate, oceans, ecosystems, health, and wellbeing.

  • Carbon180: Donate Here

    Carbon180 is a new nonprofit organization that aims to champion carbon removal solutions through science. They are on a mission to build a prosperous economy that removes more carbon from the atmosphere than we emit.

  • Evergreen Collaborative: Donate Here

    Evergreen Collaborative is a new advocacy organization with some of the most prominent scientists and policymakers working for better climate policy and environmental justice. They aim to build support for an all-out national mobilization to defeat the climate crisis and create 8 million jobs in a clean energy economy.

  • Sunrise Movement: Donate Here

    The Sunrise Movement is a youth activist organization united to stop climate change, end the corrupting influence of fossil fuel executives on politics, and elect leaders who stand up for the health and wellbeing of all people.
